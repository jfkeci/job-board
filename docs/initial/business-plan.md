# Job Advertisement Platform - Business Plan

## Executive Summary

A dual-sided job advertisement marketplace connecting employers (B2B) with job seekers (B2C). The platform monetizes through tiered job posting packages and promotional add-ons, enabling businesses to reach qualified candidates while providing job seekers free access to opportunities.

---

## Business Model

### Revenue Streams

#### 1. Job Advertisement Tiers

| Tier | Price | Features |
|------|-------|----------|
| **Basic** | €68 | Standard job posting with unlimited description length. No premium visibility features. |
| **Standard** | €387 | Highlighted company logo within listing. Access to 10 candidate CVs from database. Unlimited description length. |
| **Premium** | €570 | All Standard features plus enhanced visibility in search results and category listings. |
| **Exclusive** | €1,022 | Maximum visibility and placement. Featured position in relevant categories. Priority support. All Premium features included. |

#### 2. Promotional Add-ons

Optional social media promotion packages to extend reach:

| Channel | Price | Benefit |
|---------|-------|---------|
| **LinkedIn** | +€50 | Reach passive professional candidates |
| **Instagram** | +€50 | Target younger, active audience |
| **Facebook** | +€50 | Broad reach on most popular social network |

*Add-ons can be combined with any tier for maximum exposure.*

## Target Users

### B2B (Employers)
- Small and medium enterprises (SMEs)
- Large corporations with HR departments
- Recruitment agencies
- Startups seeking talent

### B2C (Job Seekers)
- Active job seekers
- Passive candidates open to opportunities
- Career changers
- Recent graduates

---

## Core Features

### Employer Dashboard (B2B)
- Job posting creation and management
- Tier selection and payment processing
- Applicant tracking system (ATS)
- CV database access (tier-dependent)
- Analytics and performance metrics
- Company profile management
- Invoice and billing history

### Job Seeker Portal (B2C)
- Job search with filters (location, industry, salary, etc.)
- Profile and CV builder
- Application tracking
- Job alerts and notifications
- Saved jobs and searches
- Company reviews and insights

### Public Job Board
- Searchable job listings
- Category and industry browsing
- Location-based search
- Featured/promoted listings display

---

## Value Proposition

### For Employers
- Access to qualified candidate pool
- Scalable pricing based on hiring needs
- CV database access for proactive recruiting
- Social media amplification options
- Simple posting and management interface

### For Job Seekers
- Free access to all job listings
- Easy application process
- Profile visibility to employers
- Career resources and tools

---

## Competitive Advantages

1. **Transparent tiered pricing** - Clear value at each level
2. **CV database access** - Proactive recruiting capability
3. **Social media integration** - Extended reach beyond platform
4. **Dual-sided marketplace** - Network effects benefit both parties

## Risk Considerations

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|------------|
| Low initial supply (jobs) | Seed with aggregated listings, offer discounts to early employers |
| Low initial demand (seekers) | SEO optimization, content marketing, social media presence |
| Price sensitivity | Maintain competitive entry tier, demonstrate ROI |
| Competition from established players | Focus on user experience, niche positioning, local market expertise |
